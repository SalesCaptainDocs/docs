## Before You Begin

Before connecting Gmail, make sure:

- You have access to the Gmail account you want to connect
- **2-Step Verification** is enabled on your Google account (required to generate an App Password)

## Connect Gmail

Follow the steps below to connect your Gmail account to SalesCaptain.

### Step 1: Open Gmail Integration

1. Log in to your **SalesCaptain** dashboard.
2. Go to **Settings → Integrations → Gmail**.
3. Select **Gmail** and click **Connect**.

A popup will appear asking for your Gmail address and a verification code.

![1 Jpg](/images/1.jpg.jpeg)

### Step 2: Open Google Account Settings

1. Open a new browser tab.
2. Click on **Manage your Google Account**.
3. Ensure you are logged in with the same Gmail address you want to connect.

### Step 3: Generate an App Password

1. In your Google Account settings, search for **App passwords**.

   ![3 Jpg](/3.jpeg)
2. Open **App passwords** under the Security section.

   <Note>
     **Note:** If the _App passwords_ option is not visible, enable **2-Step Verification**, then search again.
   </Note>
   ![6 Jpg](/6.jpeg)
4. Enter a custom name such as `SalesCaptain`.

   ![2 Jpg](/2.jpeg)
4. Click **Create**.
5. Google will generate a **16-character App Password**.

   ![4 Jpg](/4.jpeg)

### Step 4: Complete the Connection

1. Copy the generated **App Password**.
2. Return to the **SalesCaptain – Connect Gmail** popup.
3. Enter:
   - Your **Gmail address**
   - The **16-character App Password** in the verification code field

     ![5 Jpg](/5.jpeg)
4. Click **Connect**.

Once verified, your Gmail account will be successfully connected to SalesCaptain.

### Step 5: Connect Your Conversation Profile

After connecting Gmail, you must link it to your conversation profile to start using email inside the Inbox.

1. Click the link below:

   https://app.salescaptain.com/dashboard/settings/conversation-profile
2. Select the conversation profile you want to use.
3. Assign the connected **Gmail account** to the profile.
4. Save your changes.

   ![7 Jpg](/7.jpeg)

Once this is done, you can start using the **email feature directly from the Inbox** for customer conversations.

## Best Practices

- Never share your App Password with anyone.
- You can revoke the App Password anytime from your Google Account settings.
- If you change your Google security settings, reconnect Gmail if required.
- Test sending and receiving an email after connection to ensure everything works correctly.

## You’re All Set

Your Gmail is now connected to SalesCaptain and ready for seamless email communication from your Inbox.
