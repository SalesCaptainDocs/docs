SalesCaptain’s plans are each designed for different team sizes and goals, so you will always be able to find the one that’s just right for you. 

## Step 1: Visit the Sign-Up Page

Go to[ ](https://salescaptain.com?utm_source=chatgpt.com)[<u>salescaptain.com</u>](http://salescaptain.com) and select **Get Started** or **Sign Up** from the homepage. You’ll be redirected to the signup portal where you can create your account in just a few minutes.

## Step 2: Enter Your Account Details

Fill in the required information to create your account:

- **Full Name** – This will appear on messages and internal communications.
- **Email Address** – Used as your primary login and for all notifications.
- **Phone Number** – Connects your account to SalesCaptain’s messaging and calling features.
- **Password** – Create a strong, secure password for your account.

After entering your details, select **Create Account** to continue.

## Step 3: Choose Your Plan

Once you’ve created your account, you’ll be prompted to choose a plan. Each plan offers different levels of access and tools, so you can select the one that best matches your business goals.

### Free Plan

Perfect for small teams or first-time users exploring SalesCaptain. Includes essential communication tools, one workspace, and access to basic automations and messaging. 

You can upgrade anytime or add credits as your team or message volume continue to grow.

### Business Plan

Ideal for growing businesses managing multiple locations or higher customer volumes.\
 Includes all Free Plan features plus:

- Multi-channel inbox (SMS, email, chat, and social)
- Automations and campaigns
- Call management and analytics
- Unlimited team members and integrations

### Enterprise Plan

Designed for large organizations that need advanced control, integrations, and support.\
 Includes all Business Plan features plus:

- Custom onboarding and setup
- Dedicated account manager
- Priority support and uptime SLA
- API and advanced integration access

Compare plans in detail at[ <u>salescaptain.com/pricing</u>](https://salescaptain.com/pricing?utm_source=chatgpt.com).

## Step 4: Verify Your Account

After choosing your plan, you’ll receive a verification email at the address you provided. Click the **Verify Email** link to activate your account and confirm your credentials. Once verified, you’ll be redirected to your dashboard to begin your onboarding.

If you don’t see the email within a few minutes, check your spam or promotions folder.

## Step 5: Log In for the First Time

Return to[ ](https://app.salescaptain.com)[<u>app.salescaptain.com</u>](http://app.salescaptain.com) and log in using your verified email and password. You’ll be welcomed with a guide to help you get familiar with your workspace and core features.

## Troubleshooting

- **Didn’t receive the verification email?**\
  Check your spam folder or whitelist _@_[_salescaptain.com_](http://salescaptain.com) to ensure delivery.
- **Used the wrong email?**\
  You can restart the signup process with the correct address — unused accounts will automatically expire.
- **Can’t access your account?**\
  Select **Forgot Password** on the login page to reset your credentials.
