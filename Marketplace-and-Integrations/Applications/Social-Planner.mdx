### **Connect Your Accounts**

Start by linking your **Facebook** and **Instagram** pages to SalesCaptain. This connection allows you to create, preview, and schedule posts directly from your dashboard.

### **Create a Post**

1. Open the **Social Planner** tab.
2. Select a **date** for your post.

   <Frame>
     ![social planner claendar 2.webp](/images/socialplannerclaendar2.webp)
   </Frame>
3. Choose your **social media platform**, eg. Facebook or Instagram.
4. Add your **caption** and **upload your image**.
5. Click **Schedule** to confirm.

<Frame>
  ![social planner upload.webp](/images/socialplannerupload.webp)
</Frame>

Your post will be automatically published on the selected date and platform.

### **Best Practices**

- **Plan ahead:** Use the calendar to maintain a consistent posting schedule and avoid last-minute rushes.
- **Use engaging visuals:** Posts with high-quality images tend to perform better and draw more attention.
- **Keep captions short and clear:** Highlight key information or calls to action early in your message.
- **Check your links and tags:** Ensure all URLs, hashtags, and mentions are correct before scheduling.
- **Balance frequency:** Space out your posts to maintain audience interest without overwhelming follower
